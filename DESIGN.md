### Project Overview

ShelfSpace is a social reading application that aims to bring readers together, give them the ability to track and store their digital library, and help them find their next book. The vision for ShelfSpace is to foster an interconnected community of readers, where sharing and discussing books is enjoyable, expressive, and dynamic. By introducing innovative technology into the reading space, ShelfSpace makes book discovery and discourse meaningful. ShelfSpace will be a feature-rich platform that seeks to provide a more user-friendly alternative to book discovery and discourse, while still retaining a profitable business model.

The core objective of ShelfSpace is to 
- Create a centralized reading platform that combines book tracking with social discussion
- Foster community interaction through verboses review and rating systems, threads, and social groups.
- Create a robust book-detection system powered by cutting edge AI technology.
- Build the framework for an app that grows with the community.
